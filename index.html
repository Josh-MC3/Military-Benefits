Space-Available (Space-A) Travel

FREE MILITARY FLIGHTS
✈️

Military's Best-Kept Travel Secret

Space-Available travel allows eligible military personnel and their families to fly on Department of Defense aircraft at no cost or minimal cost on a space-available basis. With over 60 AMC terminal locations worldwide, this benefit can enable international travel for essentially free — the catch is flexibility in schedule.

Key AMC Terminals for Pacific / OCONUS Travel
⚠️ Space-A Essentials
  • Sign up at the AMC terminal in person, via email, or via AMC website
  • Registration is valid for 60 days; re-register before expiration
  • Once manifested, you have priority to complete your trip home
  • Pets are generally not allowed; contact terminal for current policy
  • Passports required for international travel (even for SOFA countries)
  • OCONUS eligible: Active duty, retirees, 100% P&T disabled vets, and their dependents

State Veteran Benefits

50 STATES
💡 Key Insight: State benefits vary enormously — some states eliminate ALL property taxes for disabled veterans, while others offer nominal exemptions. Florida, Texas, and South Dakota are often cited as top veteran-friendly states. Always verify current amounts with your State VA office, as values change annually.

VA Care Abroad

OVERSEAS PATHWAYS
💡 Reality Check: There is only one VA-operated medical facility outside the United States — the Manila VA Outpatient Clinic in the Philippines. Everywhere else overseas, "VA care" means a reimbursement program (FMP) or a referral-based exam, not a VA clinic you walk into. None of these pathways currently publish an official wait time the way CONUS VA facilities do — this tab tells you that plainly wherever it's true rather than guessing a number.
⚠️ Scope Limit That Trips People Up
The Foreign Medical Program and the Manila VA Outpatient Clinic generally cover service-connected conditions only. A routine "full check-up" unrelated to a rated disability typically isn't a covered service under either — this is the single biggest misunderstanding veterans have about VA care abroad.
